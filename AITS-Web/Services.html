Services

Active Threat Prevention

In 2022, Hiscox reported that 47% of all US businesses have suffered a cyber attack in the past 12 months, with the median cost of an attack at $18,000. 40% of these attacks cost effected businesses upwards of $25,000. As the rate of cyberattacks grows over the years, it is more important than ever to have systems in place to prevent, respond to, and recover from attacks.

We offer custom and comprehensive security solutions to protect what matters most to you. In a world of increasing threats to our livelihood, we cannot afford to be passive observers.     

Data Backups

Hardware/software failure, security breaches, and employee error can cause mission-critical files to suddenly be inaccessible. Without regular backup system in place, those very same files can be lost permanently. In some cases, losing these files can present a large time cost, with employees being required to start their work from scratch. On a larger computerized project, these labor costs can add up quickly.

With everything we’ve come accustomed to storing on our computers - the last thing we like to think about is all of that material vanishing into the abyss.

Break/Fix

Providing for regular, preventative maintenance of Infrastructure and hardware is often overlooked aspect of operating IT equipment. For some of the more valuable and often mission-critical pieces of equipment, blowing the dust out and checking for full functionality on a regular schedule can prevent unnecessary or unexpected equipment upgrades.

When a piece of equipment does eventually fail, we offer replacements and upgrade options, with a technician on site to ensure repairs go smoothly.

Networking Management

Computer networking - the transfer of data between connected devices - has become an integral part of modern business. Proper network management can provide for a faster and more secure network, company-wide. The functional state of your network can become your business’ biggest strength, or its greatest weakness.

Consider a morning commute: Would it be best practice for everyone to take the exact same path to work? Regardless of where they start their day?

Structured Cabling

Despite the advent of Wi-Fi and its effect on modern computing, there remains a large proportion of equipment that either functions better when plugged directly in, or plainly necessitates being plugged in directly. Network racks, for instance, are all hard wired. Wireless access points need a cable to send and receive signal from the network.

Need to make modifications to your office layout? Went to plug in a new computer, or printer, and slowly realized the nearest open port is halfway across the office? We can help! We got our start in structured cabling, and are happy to help with your needs!

Remote Monitoring

Remote monitoring of networked devices allows for 24/7 notification of present or impending faults in hardware or software. Error reports help our team identify solutions to problems. In some cases, we are able to offer solutions to problems before they grow into headaches that affect your workflow.

Remote Monitoring can alert us to hardware or software changes in the device - allowing us to proactively secure your device from threats. Minimally, it provides a record of change, making the troubleshooting process faster and more effective.
